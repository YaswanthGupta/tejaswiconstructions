∻∻ About Us ∻∻


Tejaswi Constructions is established in the year 2018 and One of the leading real estate development company in India. We have projects across the Andhra Pradesh to provide urbanised living spaces to all. We work for sustainable living and espouse green design. We create boutique projects that focus on delivering promises, on time, and with the best design, architecture, planning, construction quality, and thoughtful attention to detail.

We also have another company "Tejaswi Electricals" which offers a variety of electrical services for both residential and commercial properties, including upgrades, repairs, replacements, and installations. Please visit the website Tejaswi Electricals for more details.

∻∻ Property ∻∻


Apartments



Villas



Tejaswi Constructions offers luxurious Apartments and Villas that are carefully designed to address the needs of a quality contemporary lifestyle. Here nature’s vast green cover is ever ready to play its tunes and lead you into a state of calm, while the resort-style amenities are a perfect break from your everyday routine. With over 80% open green space, bring the tranquillity of nature into your home at Tejaswi Properties. Designed to let in natural light and cross ventilation, with breath-taking views, each home is an homage to the best that nature has to offer. Health friendly indoors, energy efficient lighting, low flow water fixtures and rainwater harvesting are just a few of the green initiatives. Enjoy the luxury and serenity of life in the lap of nature, while being only a short distance away from the attractions of the city. Shopping malls, schools and commercial districts are all easily accessible. Enjoy a lifestyle crafted exclusively for you only at Tejaswi Properties.
